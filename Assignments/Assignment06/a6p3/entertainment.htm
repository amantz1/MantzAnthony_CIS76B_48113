Bajuco

One of this month's featured artist is "Bajuco". As Bajuco continues to joyously blend the sounds and styles of different cultures, they hope to bring their listeners together with the same admiration, respect, and celebration for the world's musical diversity. Blending together the energy and sound of diverse musical influences, Bajuco bridges the gap between world and jazz genre. Please see the right-hand sidebar for a sample of their music.

Harp Wars

Remember the Cantina Band from Star Wars? Ever imagined how that would sound on a harp? Well of course you didn't, but here's someone who did. Another of this month's featured artists is "Harp Wars". This young solo artist plays a mean harp. And I don't mean harmonica either. He plays familiar contemporary songs in a way that might just drag that harp kicking and screaming back into the 21st century. Check out his music video in the right-hand sidebar.
